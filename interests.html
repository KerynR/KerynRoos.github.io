Hobbies & Interests


When I am not developing new IT skills, I spend my free time outdoors.

Hover over the photos to gain a little more insight.


I am very passionate about horses and I spend the majority of my free time riding and competing. I have competed in numerous provincial and national competitions over the last 16 years. Horses have taught me Empathy, Patience, Perserverance and Team-work. They have shown me that no matter how tough it gets, the thing that matters most is getting back up again.


I have always enjoyed hiking and being cutoff from the world, whenever possible. I have complete both one and multi day hikes. The highlight was a four day hike I completed in the Drakensberg, Injisuthi Region, where I summited 'The Judge' mountain peak. (Which is where this photo was taken.)


I have always felt at home not being at home. After high school I decided to take a year off to travel. In 2016, I spent 2 months travelling and camping Europe. I was fortunate enough to visit 17 different countries. This photo was taken at the top of the JungfrauJoch in the Swiss Alphs, 3454 meters above sea level.
